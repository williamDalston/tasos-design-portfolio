🌸 Meet Tamar

The Story of BEPO

Tamar Tsiklauri is more than a designer; she is a builder of communities and a keeper of stories. Her journey from fashion design student to Executive Director of the Aragvi Local Action Group has been guided by a deep-seated belief in the power of culture, community, and female strength.

For years, Tamar coordinated community-led development programs, focusing on empowering local groups and supporting women's entrepreneurship. This experience taught her that true empowerment isn't just about financial independence—it's about honoring heritage, finding strength in shared experiences, and creating a sense of belonging.

The Birth of BEPO

BEPO is the evolution of Tamar's life's work. The Rain Bag, with its Georgian motifs and designs inspired by the stories of women, is not just a product; it's a physical representation of her philosophy. "Every fold, every pattern, every stitch carries a narrative of resilience and beauty, just as Tamar's career has unfolded to protect and empower her community."
