üå∏ Meet Tamar

Heritage Protection Through BEPO

Tamar Tsiklauri combines her expertise in fashion design with her passion for cultural preservation and women's empowerment. As Executive Director of the Aragvi Local Action Group, she has dedicated her career to protecting heritage while empowering women through community development.

Through her work with community-led development programs, Tamar learned that true empowerment comes from connecting women to their cultural roots while providing practical tools for daily life. This insight led to the creation of BEPO - waterproof bags that protect your valuables while celebrating your heritage.

The Birth of BEPO

BEPO represents the perfect fusion of Tamar's dual passions: practical protection and cultural preservation. Each waterproof bag features authentic Georgian motifs that tell stories of women's strength, creating functional art that protects your valuables while connecting you to your heritage.
